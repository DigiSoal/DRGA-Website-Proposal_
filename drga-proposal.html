Website Design & Digital Ecosystem for Dr Gwen Ramokgopa

 
 
   

Project Overview

   

This proposal outlines the design and deployment of a new, secure digital ecosystem for Dr Gwen Ramokgopa. The project will establish a professional online presence with two scalable WordPress websites and a cohesive visual identity.

   
         
  • Dr Gwen & Associates: A corporate site at www.drga.co.za to showcase professional services and expertise.
    •      
  • Dr Gwen Ramokgopa Foundation: A non-profit site at www.drgwenramokgopafoundation.org to highlight community initiatives and impact.
    •    
   
      Estimated Total: R 20,940 (incl. design, hosting, CID, and integrations)    
 
 
   

Visual & Brand Identity

   

We will create a consistent and professional look across all platforms to build a strong brand presence. This includes:

   

Colour Palette: Royal Blue, Emerald Green, Soft Gold, Clean White

   

Typography: 'Playfair Display' for bold headings, 'Open Sans' for clean, readable body text

 
 
   

Detailed Scope of Work

   

Our comprehensive package includes the following deliverables for both websites:

   
         
  • Custom WordPress Theme: A modern, mobile-responsive design built for speed and security.
    •      
  • Essential Pages: Up to 5 key pages per site (e.g., Home, About, Services/Projects, Blog, Contact).
    •      
  • Professional Hosting: One year of secure, managed WordPress hosting to ensure reliability.
    •      
  • Analytics Integration: Setup of Google Analytics to track site performance and visitor behaviour.
    •      
  • Contact & Forms: Implementation of a secure contact form to capture leads and inquiries.
    •      
  • Basic Search Engine Optimization (SEO): On-page optimization for key pages to improve visibility.
    •    
 
   
   

Project Timeline & Process

   

We'll follow a clear, three-phase process to ensure a rapid and timely launch. The total project duration is approximately 3-4 weeks.

   
     

Phase 1: Planning & Design (1 week)

     

Phase 2: Development & Integration (2-3 weeks)
